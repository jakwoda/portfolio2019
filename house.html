Road to House of Lucy

House of Lucy is an informal collective based around electronic music scene. It formed naturally over time through making music, videos, gatherings. Below I will try to show you briefly the journey we had past two years.

diamond sutra

When Matt told me about his ambient album telling a story of enlightenment I already knew I want to make it visual. We tried to think how to tell both personal and universal story. We decided to disguise him behind a morphsuit. We took the green screen attire and travelled Poland looking for places that tell a story of lonely wanderer.

During those trips we got to know each other better and started working on music projects together.

polski dom

First mini-album we conceptualised during the trip was polski dom which means polish house. It was aimed to be an album telling a story of 5 houses. Each of house songs has a video that corresponds to depicted house. It got released through regime brigade label.

lucyna 2019

We had a tradition of organising a party in the middle of the year in opposition to New Years Eve. We called it Lucy’s from namesday. 2019 edition of was a non profit charity party which brought together over 200 of people. With 20 dj’s playing non stop for one 24 hours, 6 live acts, place for graffiti artists, skateboarders and everyone else; we realised our utopian dream in a garden of a suburban house.

house of lucy

We also started making music as House of Lucy at some point. Right now we made 2 albums together. Our Idea is that anything is possible. We try to show it in our music which is both inclusive and exclusive. Both meditative and dancable. It’s outsider music that might appeal to wider audience. The cube at Lucyna was a temple dedicated to that feeling. We then tried to simulate the house of Lucy as a cube constructed from wood in a smaller scale . We then put on an exhibition in Kunstcenen.xyz in Copengagen with corresponding text and tools to make your own.
