tryboarding

The term originated in the nineties around the skateboard community. It describes the act of trying to perform an evolution for a long time.A lot of people think that skateboarders usually land their tricks. But it’s quite the opposite. Failing, getting up and doing. It all over again is what happens most of the time.

social media

First thing to do was film and publish tryboarding through internet. Gaining audience; showcasing fresh tries on the streets that make skaters look at it differently.

videos

Focusing on the process of trying rather the actually succesful tricks. When 5-0 try boarding lunched over 2 years ago; It aimed to document how skateboarding really looks. As it turned out try boarding was a great educational tool That was interesting even for non-skaters. We started to release longer ambient-like almost Lifestyle videos documenting various trips. Creating alternative skate videos

Zine

After that we made a zine about places where you can practice tryboarding. There are few well known to skaters. Each one consists off picture, title, link to google street view, suggestions of tricks you can try there.
5-0 is a skateboarding trick 5-0 is slang for police Exploring that relation was main theme of my photography diploma For which I have did concrete frames for my pictures of policeman on try boarding spots.

exhibition

After that we decided to make an Tryboarding exhibition. Due to it’s unique location and climate we wanted to make it In Osiadle Domków Fińskich na jazdowie. It all worked out. Aim of the exhibition was to show the history of try boarding We also wanted for people experience it personally through Creating pieces of art on the spot. We ensured that the atmosphere does not exclude and Makes for a good place to explore, creat and try. We proved that ideas discovered in try boarding can be Transferred to other areas in life and make all more enjoyable.

competition

Later we organised a try boarding competition. It was based around a theme of being deathly. Everybody being a winner. It was a manifest in itselff. We mad a 3d printed trophy. To be exact half of a trophy/

Website

We are making a website and a community to make tryboarding worldwide. More info soon!
